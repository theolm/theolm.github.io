Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2025

1. Data Collection

VertiPro does not collect any personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.

We only collect anonymous analytics data, such as usage and performance metrics, to improve the app experience. We use Firebase Analytics and related services provided by the Firebase platform.

2. Permissions Used

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera: To record videos used for jump measurement.
  • Storage: To save and access videos locally on your device.
All videos are processed locally on the user's device. No videos are uploaded to external servers.

3. Use and Sharing of Data

Data collected through Firebase is used solely to analyze and improve the app.
No data is shared with third parties for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes.

4. Payments

VertiPro is a paid app with access unlocked through purchase via official app store platforms:

  • Google Play Store (Android)
  • Apple App Store (iOS)
The app does not process or store payment information. All transactions are handled by the app stores.

5. Minimum Age

The app does not have a minimum age requirement, but we recommend parental guidance for users under 13 years of age.
